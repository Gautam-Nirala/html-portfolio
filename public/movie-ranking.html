The Best Movies According to Bollywood IMDb

My top 3 movies of all-time.

12th Fail

The real life story of IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma and IRS officer Shraddha Joshi.

Sam Bahadur

Revolves around the high and low points of Sam Manekshaw who became the first Indian Army officer to be promoted to the rank of Field Marshal.

Laapata Ladies

The misadventures of two young brides who get lost from the same train. From mistaken identities to laugh-out-aloud escapades, the ensuing chaos guarantees laughter galore and heartwarming moments.
